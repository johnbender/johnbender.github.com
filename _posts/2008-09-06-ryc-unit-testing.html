Well I just resurrected it and I think it could be useful for people working with C. Basically it just does all the dirty work of taking your C methods and making them available for Ruby via a bundle file. The next step is to build out a Ruby unit test script with stubs for each of the methods in the bundle, thus making it very easy to unit test your C code. Check it out here.
